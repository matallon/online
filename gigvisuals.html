Gig visuals

my VJ work

Real time graphics for concerts. These include camera and audio reactive shaders as well as immersive 3D environments, which in previous iterations have been projected behind the bands playing. I originally started developing these for GAFF Edinburgh gigs, but I've since been continuing to create more immersive visuals as part of the group @astridvonderhalen, which I will be able to share more of soon! Photos below are from 29/09/2023.

I'm open to working with other organisations and bands too if you're looking for real time visuals for your event; I can create new custom ones or I have a collection of the ones I've already made linked below. I would prefer to be there for the gig in order for everything to run as well as possible, especially with the audio and camera reactive graphics. I'm currently based in Leeds but am happy to travel for a gig. Please contact me with any questions and for my rates, I'm happy to answer everything and anything!
