Gig visuals

Real time camera and audio reactive shaders, projected behind the bands playing. Software originally made for GAFF Edinburgh gigs, but can be used for any performance! Photos are from 29/09/2023. At this event I also included a variety of animations so each act at the gig could select visuals that complemented their individual sound.

I'm happy to license the software for certain events/groups if you contact me, or alternatively hire me to VJ your gig!
