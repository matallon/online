Mary Tallontire

Creative Technologist and Digital Worldbuilder

I am a freelance digital creative working across various areas such as computational art, immersive storytelling, internet culture and digital worldbuilding. I’ve recently graduated from Goldsmiths University in London with a BSc in Digital Arts Computing, and I’m a specialist in digital arts with a diverse skillset.


I have experience with a vast array of software and programming languages, ranging from C++ and C# programming skills both inside and outside of game engines, AR development skills in Lens Studio and 3D modelling capabilities in Blender and Maya. My recent work has included creating immersive real time visuals for gigs and concerts as well as developing gamified filters for Snapchat.


I’m excited for the future of my work as I hope to delve more into the potential of artistic digital environments within real world contexts - particularly live events, whilst also continuing to address themes of social justice and sustainability.
